It's Minesweeper... with a twist!

The Basic Rules

Each game starts out with a grid of unmarked squares. Some squares are safe to click (revealing a number) but others are bombs! If a bomb is clicked, game over.

The number on the square is how many bombs the square is touching: to the top, bottom, left, right, and both diagonals. It's your job to use the numbers to figure out which of the unmarked squares have bombs and which are safe to click.

If you suspect a square hides a bomb, then click the Flag button to mark it. Once all bombs are flagged, game won!

The Twist

Before you play, select a game theme. Each has its own story line:

Watch out! There's monsters lurking in the woods.

You robbed a bank! Now sneak away from the cops.

Gross! Skunks in the area. Catch them before they spray.
